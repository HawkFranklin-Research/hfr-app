[FULL DESCRIPTION] (draft)
HawkFranklin is an internal clinical research application for structured data collection, expert review, and project-based study participation.

The app is designed for clinicians and physicians who contribute labeled judgments, review cases, and participate in active research programs organized by HawkFranklin Research.

Current starter build capabilities:

- managed sign-in and onboarding placeholders
- a project dashboard with active research programs
- a flashcard-style dermatology review flow
- structured answer capture for each reviewed case
- per-user counters for reviewed and submitted cases

The long-term goal is to support decentralized, expert-driven research workflows where physicians contribute judgments on curated case material in a standardized mobile experience.

This application is intended for internal and controlled research use.
